The Last Caesar Salad Recipe You'll Ever Need

Description

This unbelievable, restaurant-quality, creamy Caesar salad dressing will make you swear off anything store-bought again. Great for your classic Caesar salad, or as a dip for all kinds of veggies.

To serve, toss with chopped romaine in a salad bowl and shave Parmesan cheese on top of salad; season with salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Prep Time: 20 mins Additional Time: 1 hr Total Time: 1 hr 20 mins

Servings: 4 Yield: 4 salmon cakes
