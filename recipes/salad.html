The Last Caesar Salad Recipe You'll Ever Need

Description

This unbelievable, restaurant-quality, creamy Caesar salad dressing will make you swear off anything store-bought again. Great for your classic Caesar salad, or as a dip for all kinds of veggies.

Prep Time: 20 mins Additional Time: 1 hr Total Time: 1 hr 20 mins

Servings: 4 Yield: 4 salmon cakes

Ingredients

  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped, or to taste
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese plus extra for serving
  • 1/4 cup half-and-half
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • Romaine
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Combine anchovy fillets with garlic in a food processor and pulse several times to form a paste. Process mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese, half-and-half, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and Worcestershire sauce with anchovy mixture until dressing is creamy.
  2. Refrigerate for 1 hour or more.
  3. To serve, toss with chopped romaine in a salad bowl and shave Parmesan cheese on top of salad; season with salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
